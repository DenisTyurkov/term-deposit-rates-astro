### Risks & considerations for NZ term deposits

Standard risks associated with New Zealand term deposits:

**Rate fixation risk**: Locked rates provide certainty but may limit benefits from rising interest environments.

**Inflation impact**: Real returns may be reduced if inflation exceeds deposit interest rates.

**Liquidity restrictions**: Funds remain committed for the agreed term, with penalties for early access typically applying.

**Institutional considerations**: Check current New Zealand deposit protection arrangements applicable to Bank of Baroda. understand regulatory protections for your deposits.

**Alternative investment opportunities**: Capital in term deposits cannot be deployed in potentially higher-yielding alternatives.
